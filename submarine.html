ABOUT

Record Label & Party Session founded by group of young and creative people from Sarajevo. The Submarine is a project that has been made by young, creative and ambitious people who are trying to, by doing what they love, create quality domestic products sailing through music, design and marketing zones of interest.

For four years of existence, Submarine becomes one of the biggest electronic label brand in Bosnia and Herzegovina, and with quality music, interesting work and way of promotion, it grabbed the attention of lot of international artists, DJs and fans. In the 2017. Submarine Vibes released first Hibrid's album "Izgubljeni"​ on CD. In the selection of Mixmag Adria, Submarine Vibes has been named as one of the best 10 regional labels, and the only one from Bosnia and Herzegovina.

Label roster includes more than 200 artists and DJs, and some of them are Martin Roth, Powel, Jonas Saalbach, PHCK, Hibrid, Rauschhaus, Franz Alice Stern, MockBeat, Sam Pauli, Marcus Sur, Timboletti, Shosho, Arara, Cliff De Zoete, Mark Slee, Philipp Kempnich, Jelly For The Babies, Formel, Addex, Julian Wassermann, Soul Of Void, Ki.Mi., Roby Deep, Marymoon, Elfenberg, Ben Gomori, Martin Kremser, A Skitzo, 2Odd, Sijah & Kolenda, Pammin, Armen Miran, Victor Norman, The Foreigners, and many more.
